About TFM

Aviation at the
Highest Standard

Total Flight Management Incorporated is a VATSIM virtual airline operating as a Part 135 on-demand air carrier. Based at Teterboro Airport (KTEB), we operate turbojet aircraft for virtual charter flights on the VATSIM network.

Our operations mirror real-world FAA Part 135 standards in every detail — from dispatch procedures and flight releases, to crew qualifications and safety management. Certificate No. JDJZ2030, overseen by POI Jay Auslander at the Teterboro FSDO.
