SimBridge
For Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 / 2024
SimBridge is a lightweight Windows app that bridges MSFS and AeroVenture over a local WebSocket connection. Runs silently in the system tray.
Windows 10/11 ~12 MB localhost:2025

XPlane Connect Plugin
For X-Plane 12
XPlane Connect is an open-source X-Plane plugin by NASA that exposes sim data over UDP/WebSocket. Drop it into your X-Plane plugins folder.
Windows / Mac / Linux localhost:2026
↗ Get XPlane Connect (GitHub)
Setup Guide
🛩️ Microsoft Flight Simulator
1
Click Download SimBridge above, grab SimBridge.exe from the releases page, and run it.
2
Launch SimBridge before starting MSFS. Confirm the tray icon shows green.
3
Start MSFS and load any aircraft into a flight. SimBridge auto-connects to the sim.
4
Return here and click Connect MSFS. The status dot will turn green.
5
Accept a charter — SimLink will monitor your position and unlock payment on arrival.
✈️ X-Plane 12
1
Download XPlane Connect from GitHub. Extract the XPlaneConnect folder.
2
Copy the folder to X-Plane 12/Resources/plugins/
3
Launch X-Plane 12 and load any aircraft. The plugin activates automatically on port 2026.
4
Return here and click Connect X-Plane. The status dot will turn green.
5
Accept a charter — SimLink will monitor your position and unlock payment on arrival.
⚠️ Charters require SimLink. After accepting a charter, depart from within 30 NM of the origin airport and land within 20 NM of the destination to collect payment. Forfeiting an accepted charter costs you 25% of its value.
